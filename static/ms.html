Money System

If you win a round as CT you earn 3400$. If you win a round as T without a bomb plant you receive 3400$, and with a bomb plant 4200$. If the round was lost but a bomb plant still was archived you are rewarded with a bonus of 800$. Losing a round will reward your team with 1400$, this amount will increase with 500$ for each consecutive lost round.

First 1400$
Second 1900$
Third 2400$
Fourth 2900$
Fifth 3400$

You will personally receive money after each round based on the kills you made in the round. The amount will depend on the type of weapon used. You will not benefit from the win/lost bonus if you survive a round as T and your team lost the round, without a bomb plant.
